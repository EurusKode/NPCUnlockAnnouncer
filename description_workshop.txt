[h1]NPC Unlock Announcer[/h1]
[b]Never miss a new villager again![/b]

Did the Merchant arrive while you were fighting a boss? Did you miss the chat message? Not anymore!
This mod adds a stylish, RPG-style announcement banner whenever a Town NPC (or town pet) arrives in your world.

[h2]Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Stylish Announcements:[/b] A golden banner appears at the top of the screen with the NPC's icon.
[*] [b]Unique Subtitles:[/b] Every Vanilla NPC has a custom, witty subtitle (English & Spanish).
[*] [b]Mod Compatibility:[/b] Works with modded NPCs (displays a generic "New Ally" message or uses their name).
[*] [b]Visuals:[/b] Clean, vanilla-friendly UI that fits perfectly with Terraria's aesthetic.
[/list]
